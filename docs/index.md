## üìã Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us deliver a better parking experience to you:

### **1. Account Information (Required for Sign-In)**
- **What we collect:** Name and email address via Google Sign-In
- **Why we need it:** To create your account, authenticate you securely, and personalize your experience
- **What we don't do:** We never store your Google password

### **2. Camera Access (For QR & License Plate Scanning)**
- **What we collect:** QR code scans and license plate images captured through your camera
- **Why we need it:** To enable quick check-in/check-out at parking facilities and automatic vehicle recognition
- **How we protect it:** Images are processed locally on your device. We only store the extracted QR code data or license plate number‚Äînever the actual photos
- **Your control:** You can disable camera access anytime in your device settings (scanning features won't work without it)

### **3. Location Data (For Finding Parking)**
- **What we collect:** Approximate and precise location (when you grant permission)
- **Why we need it:** To show you nearby parking spots, provide navigation, and estimate arrival times
- **How we protect it:** Location is only accessed when you actively use parking discovery features
- **Your control:** You can disable location access anytime in Settings

### **4. Payment Information (Processed Securely)**
- **What we collect:** Transaction IDs, payment status, wallet balance history
- **What we DON'T collect:** We NEVER store your full credit/debit card details, CVV, or card PIN
- **Who processes it:** All payment processing is handled by **Razorpay**, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway
- **Your security:** Your payment data is encrypted end-to-end using bank-grade security

### **5. Booking & Parking Data**
- **What we collect:** Parking lot selections, booking timestamps, check-in/check-out times, parking duration, vehicle information you add
- **Why we need it:** To manage your bookings, generate receipts, and provide booking history
- **Your benefit:** This helps us show you relevant parking recommendations

### **6. Device & Usage Analytics**
- **What we collect:** App version, device model, Android OS version, crash reports, feature usage patterns
- **Why we need it:** To fix bugs, improve app performance, and understand which features you love
- **What we don't track:** We don't track your personal browsing or activity outside Gridee
